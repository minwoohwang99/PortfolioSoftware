AutoTopster

Automatically generate your own personalized music collage based on your Spotify listening history using AutoTopster!

Many fellow music fans are familiar with the site "Topster," famous for allowing users to create collages of album covers. Although a great tradition among online music circles, it can be quite tedious to individually handpick each album cover, especially if you want to make multiple Topsters for representing different aspects of your listening. AutoTopster serves as a much faster and easier alternative to creating these collages with the added functionality of allowing your favourite artist' Spotify profile pictures to be turned into a collage as well. Rep your favourite music online using AutoTopster!

How it works: Select your collage type (songs or artists), select a grid size, and a time range, and watch your collage appear in seconds!

Python, Flask, JavaScript, HTML/CSS
